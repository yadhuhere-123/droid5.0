Genesis of DROID

DROID 5.0 is the flagship technical symposium organized by IEEE FISAT SB in collaboration with IEEE RAS SBC FISAT. Since its inception, DROID has served as a crucible for innovation, bringing together the brightest minds in robotics and automation.

Engineering Excellence

Much like the world's leading engineering brands, DROID 5.0 is built on the pillars of precision, technical rigor, and future-forward thinking. Our mission is to bridge the gap between classroom theory and industrial-grade robotics application through a 13th July 2026 technical odyssey.

The DROID Standard

The 2026 Vision

For its fifth iteration, DROID 5.0 focuses on the theme of "Autonomous Intelligence." We aim to empower delegates with the tools needed to design vehicles, drones, and industrial arms that think, learn, and act independently.
